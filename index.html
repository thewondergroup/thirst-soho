Since 1999

Established by two friends, still going strong

Established in 1999 by two friends, our humble bar has been serving the best cocktails and high-energy late night parties ever since.

From chilled catch-ups over delicious drinks to how is it 3am already? nights, we've got you.

Join us in the main bar for pouring cocktails (including our renowned Stupid Hour) and plenty of spirits. Or check out our chill-out lounge space if you want to sit and talk or perfect for birthday hires. Outside, we have a heated, covered terrace with comfy seating and its own bar — rain or shine.
